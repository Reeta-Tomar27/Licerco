Licenses Available

Trade License

A trade license is required for operating various types of businesses, including retail shops, restaurants, hotels, beauty salons, manufacturing units, and warehouses. It ensures compliance with local regulations regarding safety, sanitation, and business operations.

  • Typical Businesses: Retail shops, restaurants, hotels, beauty salons, manufacturing units, warehouses
  • Regulatory Authority: Local Municipal Corporation
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Fire Department NOC

A Fire Department No Objection Certificate (NOC) is required for establishments such as hotels, shopping malls, manufacturing plants, hospitals, educational institutions, and entertainment venues. It ensures compliance with fire safety standards and the presence of fire prevention equipment.

  • Typical Businesses: Hotels, shopping malls, manufacturing plants, hospitals, educational institutions, entertainment venues
  • Regulatory Authority: Local Fire Department
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Environmental Clearance

Environmental clearance is required for industries such as manufacturing, mining, power generation, construction, and waste management. It ensures compliance with environmental regulations and the mitigation of environmental impacts.

  • Typical Businesses: Manufacturing industries, mining operations, power plants, construction projects, waste management facilities
  • Regulatory Authority: State Pollution Control Board, Ministry of Environment
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Health License

A Health License is required for establishments involved in healthcare services, including hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, food processing units, childcare centers, and fitness centers. It ensures compliance with health and safety standards.

  • Typical Businesses: Hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, food processing units, childcare centers, fitness centers
  • Regulatory Authority: Health Department
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Food License

A Food License is required for establishments involved in food services, including restaurants, food manufacturing units, catering services, food transportation, and street food vendors. It ensures compliance with food safety standards.

  • Typical Businesses: Restaurants, food manufacturing units, catering services, food transportation, street food vendors
  • Regulatory Authority: Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Liquor License

A Liquor License is required for establishments involved in the sale and distribution of alcoholic beverages, including bars, liquor stores, hotels, clubs, and event organizers. It ensures compliance with liquor laws and regulations.

  • Typical Businesses: Bars, liquor stores, hotels, clubs, event organizers
  • Regulatory Authority: Excise Department
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Transportation Permits

Transportation permits are required for businesses involved in logistics, trucking, bus operations, taxi services, tour operations, and vehicle rental. They ensure compliance with transportation regulations and safety standards.

  • Typical Businesses: Logistics companies, trucking services, bus operators, taxi services, tour operators, vehicle rental businesses
  • Regulatory Authority: Transport Department
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection may be required
Learn More
Construction Permits

Construction permits are required for businesses involved in construction activities, including contractors, real estate developers, civil engineering firms, architects, renovation contractors, and interior design firms. They ensure compliance with construction regulations and safety standards.

  • Typical Businesses: Construction contractors, real estate developers, civil engineering firms, architects, renovation contractors, interior design firms
  • Regulatory Authority: Local Municipal Corporation, Building Permit Authority
  • Requirement: Approval may involve physical site inspection
Learn More
Entertainment License

An Entertainment License is required for establishments involved in providing entertainment services, such as cinemas, theaters, amusement parks, event management companies, and nightclubs. It ensures compliance with entertainment regulations and safety standards.

  • Typical Businesses: Cinemas, theaters, amusement parks, event management companies, nightclubs
  • Regulatory Authority: Entertainment Licensing Board
  • Requirement: Physical site inspection
Learn More
Public Events Permissions

Permissions for public events are required for organizing festivals, trade shows, sporting events, cultural events, charity fundraisers, parades, and other public gatherings. They ensure compliance with event regulations and safety standards.

  • Typical Events: Festivals, trade shows, sporting events, cultural events, charity fundraisers, parades, public gatherings
  • Regulatory Authority: Local Municipal Corporation or Event Management Authority
  • Requirement: Approval may involve physical site inspection
